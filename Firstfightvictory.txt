The Goblin lets out a final snarl before collapsing at your feet.

Silence returns to the Entrance Hall.

For a moment, you simply stand there, catching your breath.
The reality settles in -

You have drawn first blood.

The fortress is no longer untouchable.
Its monsters are not invincible.

You were sent here for a reason.
And this victory, small as it may seem, proves you are capable.

But the air has not grown lighter.
If anything… it feels as though the dungeon has noticed you.

This was only a scout.
Stronger creatures lurk deeper within.

Ahead lies the Hall of Fame -
a chamber once built to honour heroes.

Now, it waits to test one.

You tighten your grip on your weapon.

Your journey has truly begun.
